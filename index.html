A historic space for your historic celebration

Celebrate your special event in style when you choose Olde Wicks Factory Special Event Center in Highland, IL. Our venues are located in a 100-year-old building, formerly the Pipe Organ Company, and feature stunning stained glass, exposed brick, chandeliers, and beautiful outdoor space. With three distinct spaces to choose from, your wedding venue, event space, or reception hall will be everything you envisioned and more.

Redesigned to accommodate a variety of gatherings, including birthday parties, weddings, baby showers, and corporate events, we have the perfect banquet hall for your celebration or gathering. We have space for anywhere from 120 people to 350, ensuring that no matter how large your group, there’s space for all in our stunning event center.

If you’re on the market for a wedding venue that is distinct, spectacular, and has everything you need to ensure your special day goes off without a hitch, look no further! Not only do we have the ideal spaces for your ceremony and reception, but we also offer a bridal suite designed for hair, makeup, and photos. You and your bridesmaids will have all the space you need to get ready and your photographer will have the perfect opportunities to get those “getting ready” photos every lady loves.
